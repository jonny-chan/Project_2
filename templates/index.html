Is it worth the cost?

It’s commonly known that California, and the Bay Area in particular, offers some of the best, highest-paying jobs in the nation. It’s also commonly known, however, that the Bay Area boasts some of the most expensive real estate in the world. For young professionals who are looking to settle down, this raises an important question: “Is the Bay Area salary worth it?”. We’ve attempted to answer that question by looking at the average sale price for homes in five major American cities.
